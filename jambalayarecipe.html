Jambalaya

Description

Made with rice, meat/seafood, and vegetables, Jambalaya is a New Orleans home favorite dish. This delicious Southern cuisine dish starts with the Cajun holy trinity: flavor base of onion, bell peppers, and celery.

Ingredients

  • Oil:Chicken and andouille sausage sauteed in peanut oil.
  • Sausage.
  • Chicken: Boneless, skinless chicken breasts into 1-inch pieces.
  • Spices with fresh garlic, red pepper flakes, salt, pepper, hot sauce, Worcestershire sauce.
  • Onion, green bell peppers, and celery.
  • Chicken broth

Steps

  1. Cook the meat.
  2. Cook the vegetables.
  3. Add the rice.

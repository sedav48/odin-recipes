Mini Sweet Potato Pie

Description

Easy to make and small enough for small palates, this quick dessert is a winner for your Thanksgiving turnout.This yummy dish can be used as an appetizer or for a holiday get together. You cannot go wrong with this delicable treat.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 pound sweet potato, peeled and diced
  • 1 (9 inch) refrigerated pie crust
  • 2 egg whites
  • 3/4 cup evaporated skim milk
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons brown sugar
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/4 cup halved cranberries (Optional)

Steps

  1. Place sweet potato in a saucepan with just enough wayter to cover. Bring to a boil; cook until fork-tender, about 5 minutes. Drain and mash potatoes with a fork or potato masher.
  2. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F(220 degrees C)
  3. Divide pie crust into 24 small balls. Press them into the cups of two 12-cup mini muffin pans to make tartlet shells. Set aside
  4. Spoon sweet potato into blender or food processor. Add evaporated milk, egg whites, white sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves. Puree until smooth. Spoon about 1 tablespoon filling into each tart shell.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into a tart comes out clean, about 10 minutes. Cool in the pans over a wire rack. Garnish each tart with a cranberry half before serving.
